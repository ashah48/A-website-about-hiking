What is Thru-Hiking?

Thru-Hiking is hiking the entire trail. On the White Mountains presidential range, I met Jay from Jay Wonders Out, a youtube channel where Jay vlogs his thru-hiking experiences. One day I too would like to thru hike the entire AT which is close to 2200 miles long! It usually takes 5-7 months to thru-hike the AT so makes sure you are in good shape so you can save as much time as possible! Creating a budget for food resupplies is very important.
